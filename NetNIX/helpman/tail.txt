        NAME
            tail - show last lines of a file

        SYNOPSIS
            tail [-n N] <file> [file2...]

        OPTIONS
            -n N        Show last N lines (default 10)
            -N          Short form (e.g. -20 for last 20 lines)

        SEE ALSO
            head, cat
