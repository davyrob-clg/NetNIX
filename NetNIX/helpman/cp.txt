        NAME
            cp - copy files and directories

        SYNOPSIS
            cp [-r] <source> <dest>

        OPTIONS
            -r, -R      Copy directories recursively

        EXAMPLES
            cp file.txt backup.txt
            cp -r /home/alice /home/alice.bak

        SEE ALSO
            mv, rm
