        NAME
            head - show first lines of a file

        SYNOPSIS
            head [-n N] <file> [file2...]

        OPTIONS
            -n N        Show first N lines (default 10)
            -N          Short form (e.g. -5 for first 5 lines)

        EXAMPLES
            head /var/log/syslog
            head -n 3 /etc/motd

        SEE ALSO
            tail, cat
