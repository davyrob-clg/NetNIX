        DU(1)                     NetNIX Manual                     DU(1)

        NAME
            du - estimate disk usage

        SYNOPSIS
            du [-s] [-h] [dir]

        OPTIONS
            -s          Summary (total only)
            -h          Human-readable sizes (K, M)

        EXAMPLES
            du /home
            du -sh /bin

        SEE ALSO
            df, ls
