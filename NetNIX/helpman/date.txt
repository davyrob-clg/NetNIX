        NAME
            date - print current date and time

        SYNOPSIS
            date [-u] [-I] [+%s]

        OPTIONS
            -u          UTC time
            -I          ISO 8601 date format (yyyy-MM-dd)
            +%s         Unix timestamp (seconds since epoch)

        SEE ALSO
            env, uname
